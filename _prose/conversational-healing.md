## What This Is

A self-select invitation with an air of caution. Not a wide net — a door described clearly enough that the right people recognize it.

Sustained presence with someone, where the tools shift based on what they need. Not a program. Not a course.

## Who This Is For

People with already-honed discernment who want help defining and trusting it. People who are already intrinsically motivated. People willing to allow the process to take time.

## The Structure

### 1. Consultation Call
A phone call to feel out alignment. Questions to explore:
- Why are they coming to me?
- What kinds of self-improvement have they tried? What did they like and not like?
- What practices do they already have in their lives?
- Are they willing to allow the process to take time?

### 2. First Session: Core Gift Interview
A structured interview process rooted in decades of cross-cultural collaboration — developed by western practitioners and leaders from indigenous tribes around the world. Spiritual in essence.

Scheduled as a 2-hour session: ample time for the interview and discussion afterward. The product is a statement about who they are and how they offer that to the world.

This session also serves as a test of whether we can sit in frustration and productive tension together.

### 3. Second Session
Discussion of how the gift experience lingered or didn't. Then: analysis of goals.

### 4. Ongoing Work
Built from scratch, session by session. Tools drawn from:
- Intuitive practices: tarot/oracle, art-therapy-grounded painting, poetry
- Pattern recognition: rooted in hypervigilance, reinforced by years of personal therapy, supported by a background in biological data science and data engineering
