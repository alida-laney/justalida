When I last sought out a therapist, I came into the relationship with criteria & intention, which has worked wonderfully. I honor a directed expression of needs.)</p>
        <p>Through exploration, we will find the shape of what works together. If your body needs to dance, we will dance. If your spirit needs to make art and rip it to shreds, I will provide witness for that. If you need to just GET YOUR THOUGHTS OUT, I'm absolutely here for that (because, yeah, me too).</p>
