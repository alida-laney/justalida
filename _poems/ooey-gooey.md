the waitress sets down a perfect pizza,  
still warm from the oven,  
cheese still soft.  

you ate breakfast but skipped lunch.  
you’re *just* too hungry.  
you are going to devour this.  

that anticipation;  
that sensation of a feast;  
is what i feel between my hips right now.  

my low belly grumbles with certainty.  
the meal is so close,  
only the slowness of civility restrains me now.  

i take a deep breath in,  
filling my lungs with heat.  
i exhale.  

*soon.*
