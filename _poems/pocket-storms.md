---
title: "Pocket Storms"
layout: poem
date: 2026-04-16
tags: [presence, observation, noticing, water, gratitude]
excerpt: "something's coming"
dedication: "those in the unwanted rain"
---

From my view on the hill,  
There is a valley between me  
And the neighbors.  

The air is thick and humid,  
With that *something’s coming* feel.  
I can see the rain on the opposite hill.  

Despite a few stray drops,  
I am confident the rainstorm is contained  
To the northern folks.  

I feel neither luck nor pity;  
I do not know how their crops have grown.  
All I know is  

I am grateful to be dry.
