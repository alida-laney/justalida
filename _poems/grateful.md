Gratitude sits higher in my chest than it once did.  

*Grateful*  
Is a word that once carried such  
Heavy  
Shame.  

*You should be so lucky!*  
Yelled at me through screen doors,  
Hot beer on the breath  
Or cold, cold regret.  

The mistakes of others;  
Their lives half-lived;  
Are not my burden anymore.  

I am free and weightless,  
Able to saunter and dance,  
With gratitude for the little toes who carry me,  
The legs who hold me up,  
The blood who fuels my body with oxygen.  

Life flows through me today.  
Of course I am grateful.
