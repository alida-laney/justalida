As we certainly know by now,  
Writing my poems  
And sharing my poems  
Are entirely different beasts!  

Watching the count next to the “Poetry” folder increase  
Left me with an eerie sense of  
*Who does she think she is?*  

Publishing my website, though…  
Was like seeing beams of sunlight  
Filter down through patchy clouds,  
Just after a storm -  

Potent enough  
To make you think  
You might be seeing  
Heaven.  

*mmm*  

And!  

Reading my collection is different still!  

Revisiting “endurance”  
From the actual place that woman  
Hoped to have achieved…  

I feel like a mother  
Watching her son return from a world war.  

*He is safe!*  
Whispered with simultaneous  
Relief and  
Survivor’s guilt.  

How interesting…!
