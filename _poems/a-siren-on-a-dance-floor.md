I kiss a woman in a nightclub… or,  
She kisses me.  

It’s great.  
Her body is soft.  

Her energy threatens to suck the soul right out of me,  
But I like it.  

Her siren call reminds me of my own  
Magnetism -  
The power that must have pulled her here.  

I dance with this pull until I’ve had enough.  
I finish the night with my friends.  

The next morning,  
My ego is sick.  

I am nauseous  
With reminders  

Of the man who fell in love with this  
Electromagnetic woman  
And kept her in a faraday cage.  

I hate him,  
I think.  

I see now that I was never gone,  
Only muffled.  

This beautiful woman  
on this questionable dance floor  
has kissed that proof into existence.
