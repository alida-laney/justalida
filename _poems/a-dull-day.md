Slept in,  
Rolled out of bed,  
Dressed in a hurry.  

Raced over to a reiki share —  
My first one!  
Marinated.  

Returned home,  
NAPPED with my cat,  
Cooked a weird soup.  

Walked to the local market,  
Bought expensive local milk,  
Enjoyed the humid-after-a-summer-rain air.  

Ate weird soup,  
Pork, sweet potato, cilantro, jalapeño, lentils,  
DELICIOUS!  

*Root vegetables ground you!*  
Said the reiki folks.  
I think they were right.  

Full belly,  
On the bed with my cat,  
Happy.  

Happy for the first time in a long time.
