There’s something completely novel for me  
In being allowed to be terrified.  

Every other time,  
I’ve had to summon my own strength,  
Push through,  
Fight,  
And find the pride and confidence.  

I have this commitment now,  
With a friend,  
Which means I Will Do It,  
And so I am free  
To be so fucking scared.  

This is that distribution they talk about —  
That idea that community holds us in ways  
That change individual experience.  

I’ll admit,  
It does simplify things.  

Where there used to be struggle,  
Determination,  
Triumph,  
There is now  

Relief and terror.  

But that is a new combination!  

Some day,  
God willing,  
It will just be terror
