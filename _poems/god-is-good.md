I see a family on the internet with a sick boy.  
*Pray for our son.*  

I wonder what’s wrong.  
I send them light.  

Two days pass,  
I see the update.  

Smiling parents  
Over the hospital bed.  

Dad’s caption says, of the 2 month old:  
*He is a champ.*  

*He’s in the 2% of kids with this condition.*  
*They wouldn’t have found it if the*  

*Whole days worth of procedures*  
*Had worked.*  

*He needed surgery.*  
*God is good.*  

…  
yeesh.  

I send light again.  
The boy looks exhausted.  

My brain…  
Draws a connection,  

“He is so good to me!  
I am so grateful!”  

In every journal entry, for months,  
As I was being eroded away.  

That man wasn’t good to me;  
I fear your God might be the same.  

And I fear...  
Discussing Goodness or badness  
Flattens the whole thing.  

Perhaps your beautiful boy survives and  
Lives a long, healthy life.  

I hope so,  
For your sake.  

I also think of how many women lost  
So many children  

Before the last century  
Of modern medicine.  

Are we numbing ourselves to the suffering  
By believing life,  
And God,  
Must always be Good?  

Suffering exists  
Whether we discuss it or not.  

I think we should  
Discuss it.  

I think we should...  
Dialogue with our Gods.
