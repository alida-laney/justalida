date: 2026-03-04

Stunned silence.  
Head-spinning confusion.  
Chest collapsing grief.  
Exhaustion.  

Grinning-ear-to-ear JOY!  
Mundane office job neutrality.  
Brows-furrowed determination.  
Pensive sadness.  

Unpredictable cycle  
After  
Cycle.  

The only thing that can be done  
Is allow it.  

I am fifteen feet off the beach,  
Letting the waves carry me  
Up.  
Down.  
Under.  

In.  
Out.  
Wherever they please.  

One emotion  
At a time,  
Full volume.  

I’m doing better than I ought to be  
Because I am not allowing fear  
To be the wave that overtakes me.
